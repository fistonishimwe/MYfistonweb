ICT Training & Innovation

We offer computer literacy, coding workshops, internet access, and tech mentoring for youth and entrepreneurs in Muhanga District.

Art & Craft

Our creative space supports local artisans in traditional and modern crafts. We run workshops on weaving, painting, jewelry making, and sculpture.

Umuco Nyarwanda (Rwandan Culture)

We preserve and promote Rwandan cultural values through storytelling, traditional dance, poetry, and language programs for all generations.
