How Hanga Hub Was Built

Hanga Hub Muhanga was launched through a collaboration between local youth innovators and the district government, with funding and technical support from national and international organizations. The hub was physically constructed using eco-friendly materials and community-based labor.

Version 20250

This is the 20250 strategic version of Hanga Hub, focusing on sustainable innovation, inclusive technology, and cultural preservation for the next generation.
